Quick Start Guide

  • Adjust Parameters: Use the sliders to adjust Rainfall, Temperature, Invasive Species, and Human Impact.
  • Run the Simulation: Click the "Start Simulation" button to see the ecosystem in action.
  • Pause and Resume: Click the "Pause" button or press the space bar to pause/resume the simulation.
  • Reset Simulation: Click the "Reset" button to restart the simulation with default values.
  • Toggle Grass Limit: Click the "Disable Grass Limit" button to remove the upper limit on grass population.
  • Toggle Seasons Display: Use the checkbox to show or hide seasonal changes in the background.
  • Toggle Natural Disasters: Use the checkbox to enable or disable natural disasters in the simulation.
  • Monitor Population Chart: Watch the population chart to see changes over time.
  • Achieve Objectives: Complete objectives to earn points and unlock achievements.
  • Export Data: Use the "Export CSV" button to download the simulation data for further analysis.
